High School Debate Competitions

 Hosted on 12th April,2024

A debate competition in school is an organized event where students present arguments or discuss a topic from two opposing sides. The main objective is to convince others about one's viewpoint. Debates can help students develop problem-solving and innovative thinking skills, and build links between words and ideas. They can also help students explore real-world topics and issues, engage in self-reflection, and learn from their peers.
